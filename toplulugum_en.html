SDU Software and Cyber Security Community

Our main goal is to raise awareness about cyber security, eliminate the most critical and noteworthy security issues of our age, and raise public awareness. By providing the necessary technical and theoretical training, we aim to raise individuals who have adopted the cyber security infrastructure. We are an active community organizing activities such as software classes, conferences, career days, and technical trips in our community room during the semester.

🔐 Cyber Security Summit

In this event organized by e-safe, expert speakers shared information about cyber threats and career opportunities. A CTF competition was held, and one of our members placed 3rd.

💻 Software Classes

Throughout the semester, classes such as web development, introduction to programming, and algorithms were taught in our community room with teams of maximum 20 people, aiming for our members to learn in the highest quality and most effective way.

🚀 Career Days

SDU graduates who are experts in their fields met with students and shared their experiences.
